Our Story

Chow’s Double Happiness Chilli Oil is an old family recipe that has been passed down through two generations. My great grandfather immigrated to Canada from China when he was a young man; where he became a resident cook for a family in Toronto. He was passionate to try new things and experiment with food; this led him to invent his own recipe for chilli oil.

Whenever I would go to Toronto to see my mother's side of the family; my grandfather Ken Chow would always cook dishes that his father would cook for him when he was younger. Every dish accompanied or had the chilli oil on it which enhanced the dishes with a bit of heat and extra flavour. We are now producing this recipe in the UK and are stocked in shops around England.

- J. Moxon, owner.
