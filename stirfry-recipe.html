Chicken & Noodle Stirfry

Serves: 2 adults

Cooking time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 limes, 1 juiced and 1 wedged
  • 120g egg noodles
  • 300g chicken breast, sliced 1cm thick
  • 1 small carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 small red pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded and thinly sliced
  • 1 bok choi, each leaf sliced in half
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • 30g fresh ginger, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped finely
  • Chow's Double Happiness Chilli Oil

Steps

  1. Bring a pan full of water up to a boil, cook and strain the noodles.
  2. Mix together in a small bowl the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar and lime juice.
  3. Heat up a wok or a large frying pan over a high heat with the sesame oil.
  4. Add the chicken to the pan and fry until charred all around.
  5. Once the chicken has been charred, add the carrot, red pepper, chilli and garlic and continue to fry at a high heat, shaking the wok constantly to ensure an even char.
  6. At this point add the noodles, ginger, bok choi and the mixed sauce to the pan and continue to fry over high heat for a further 2 minutes.
  7. Serve with lime wedges and large spoonfuls of our own Chow’s Double Happiness Chilli Oil.
